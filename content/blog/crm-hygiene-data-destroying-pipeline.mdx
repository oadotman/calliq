## The Anatomy of a Poisoned Pipeline

Poor **CRM data hygiene** manifests in five deadly ways:

### 1. Zombie Deals (The Walking Dead)
These are opportunities that died months ago but still shamble through your pipeline:
- No activity in 60+ days
- Contact left the company
- Budget was eliminated
- Project was canceled

**Pipeline Impact**: Inflates forecast by 30-40%

### 2. Frankenstein Records (The Monsters)
Cobbled together from multiple data sources, these records are barely recognizable:
- Three different email addresses
- Conflicting phone numbers
- Multiple company spellings
- Duplicate opportunities

**Pipeline Impact**: Wastes 5+ hours weekly per rep

### 3. Ghost Contacts (The Phantoms)
People who never existed or vanished long ago:
- Bounced emails
- Disconnected phones
- Left the company
- Never had buying authority

**Pipeline Impact**: 25% of outreach efforts wasted

### 4. Time Warp Deals (The Eternal)
Opportunities stuck in the same stage for months:
- "Negotiation" for 6 months
- "Proposal sent" for 90 days
- "Demo scheduled" from last quarter

**Pipeline Impact**: Blocks realistic forecasting

### 5. Wishful Thinking Entries (The Fantasies)
Data that reflects hope, not reality:
- Deal values pulled from thin air
- Close dates based on quota needs
- Probabilities assigned by optimism
- Next steps that never happen

**Pipeline Impact**: Creates 40% forecast variance

### The Carrot and Stick
**Attempt**: Incentives for clean data, penalties for bad
**Reality**: Reps game the system, resentment builds

**Attempt**: Data enrichment tools

**Reality**: One person can't clean faster than 20 reps can pollute
