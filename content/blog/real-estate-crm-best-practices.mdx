## The Million-Dollar CRM Framework

### 1. Lead Capture and Classification

**The Lead Hierarchy:**
```
A LEADS (Hot) - Ready in 0-30 days
- Pre-approved buyers
- Motivated sellers
- Immediate need
→ Daily contact

B LEADS (Warm) - Ready in 30-90 days
- Getting pre-approved
- Considering selling
- Researching market
→ Weekly contact

C LEADS (Nurture) - Ready in 90+ days
- Future buyers/sellers
- Investment interested
- Market watchers
→ Monthly contact

D LEADS (Database) - Relationship building
- Past clients
- Sphere of influence
- Referral sources
→ Quarterly touch
```

### 2. The Property Matching System

**Essential Fields to Track:**
- Budget range (pre-approval amount)
- Preferred neighborhoods (ranked)
- Must-haves vs nice-to-haves
- Deal breakers
- Timeline urgency
- Financing type
- Down payment ready

**The Perfect Property Alert:**
```
New Listing Match for [Client Name]

Property: 123 Main St
Price: $450,000 (within budget)
Neighborhood: Riverside (1st choice)
Bedrooms: 4 (requested 3-4)
Features matching wants:
✓ Updated kitchen
✓ Home office
✓ Good schools
✓ Large yard

Missing nice-to-haves:
- Pool
- 3-car garage

Showing availability: Tomorrow 2pm

[Schedule Showing] [Send to Client]
```

### 3. The Transaction Pipeline

**Stage-by-Stage CRM Management:**

**Buyer Pipeline:**
1. Initial Contact → Needs assessment call
2. Consultation → Buyer presentation
3. Pre-Approval → Lender connection
4. Showing Tours → Property feedback
5. Offer Writing → Terms negotiation
6. Under Contract → Inspection coordination
7. Closing Process → Timeline management
8. Post-Close → Relationship maintenance

**Seller Pipeline:**
1. Initial Inquiry → Market analysis
2. Listing Appointment → Pricing strategy
3. Preparation → Home improvements
4. Active Listing → Showing feedback
5. Offer Negotiation → Terms management
6. Under Contract → Contingency navigation
7. Closing → Final walkthrough
8. Post-Sale → Referral cultivation

### 4. The Communication Cadence

**Automated but Personal:**

**New Lead Sequence:**
- Minute 1: Auto-response acknowledgment
- Hour 1: Personal phone call
- Day 1: Welcome email with resources
- Day 3: Market report for their area
- Day 7: Check-in call
- Day 14: Featured listings
- Day 30: Market update
- Ongoing: Weekly valuable content

**Under Contract Sequence:**
- Daily: Status update (first week)
- Every 3 days: Progress report
- Weekly: Next steps reminder
- Before milestones: Preparation emails
